Hospital Information Management System (HIMS)

HIMS is our Hospital Information Management System that has comprehensive modules that support the vital healthcare services, management and admin functions. It is basically an integrated solution which is designed to provide maximum support to the management of multi-specialty hospitals.

HIMS is user-friendly, technologically updated and a web-based application. All data entries are password protected with audit security. Also, a user must have access permission with hierarchical privileges each to specific functions the user wants to use. Systems and processes are designed to be fully compliant with Data Privacy Act (DPA) or RA10173 regulations, especially the protection of medical records, patient’s medical history, and organizational, business and trade secrets.

HIMS also has the ability to be fully integrated with our Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solutions to include HR, Payroll, Accounting, and Asset Management.

Patient Management

This is where you will manage the records of the patients including their Unique ID. This will allow you to register their basic information along with their medical history. You can also put the Patient Photo Capture, Registration Card and Barcode Print in the system. It also has notifications and birthday greetings, and Care Plan, Next Visit Schedule, Vaccination and Reminder.


Physicians Management

This provides the basic information of the Physicians, and their Unique IDs. Physicians’ Specialization, Degrees, Referring Doctor, and Physician Dashboard can also be accessed in this module.
