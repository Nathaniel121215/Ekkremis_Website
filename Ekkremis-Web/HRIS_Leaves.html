EASILY VIEW THE LEAVES THE ARE PENDING FOR APPROVAL

Hassle-free leave request processing.

Once your employee confirmed their leave request, the system will automatically send an email notification to the approver. All request can be accessible under Leaves to Approve, it will show the employees’ name, leave type, description, start and end date, as well as the status. Click on the pending request, and decide whether to approve or refuse.

CONSOLIDATED DEPARTMENT LEAVES

Track attendance online and real-time.

The system also allows you to see all the consolidated leaves of your employees, and the status of each request. Description is also available and serve as the reason of their leave.
