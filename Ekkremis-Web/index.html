About the Company

EKKREMIS SOLUTIONS INC.


Ekkremis Solutions, Inc. (ESI) is an integration service provider company that holds office in Makati, City Philippines.


ESI is a Filipino-owned company and was founded by experienced Business Analysts and Information Technology professionals with background in corporate finance, human resources management and telecommunication industries in the Philippines for more than 30 mandated years of experience.

MISSION
