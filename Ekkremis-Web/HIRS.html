Human Resource Information System (HRIS)

Step up your game by using our HRIS Solution to automate your business processes and to make your business execute HR-related functions more easily and efficiently.

Flex-ERP will provide you an electronic visibility on all HR activities and processes that you want to monitor and measure. It will also enable you to take care of a number of activities, including those related to human resources - such as recruiting, applicant tracking, attendance, employee management, and payroll.

Flex-ERP is composed of dozens of interrelated and integrated modules that can be pulled together, and custom-tailored for your specific business needs. This is highly configurable and can always align to your business rules and processes. Each module has the ability to generate reports that your company might be needing.
