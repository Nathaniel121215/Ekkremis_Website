OTHER SYSTEMS

These are other systems that we offer as a single package that can help your company solve a specific problem and will improve the technology aspect of your business.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
Project management system will provide visibility on project activities, timelines, and deliverables to enable effective and efficient monitoring and management.
INVENTORY SYSTEM
Inventory System will provide visibility of all product movements and transactions, and allow you to manage and track inventory levels, materials, supplies, or final products you have on your warehouse shelf, in store, or sitting with other retailers and distributors, orders, sales, and deliveries.
CLINIC MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
Clinic Management System will help streamline your clinic workflow and operations. Doctors can check their schedule and patients can book appointments, manage prescriptions, etc. This is a lighter version of Hospital Management System with more focus on the clinic’s specialization.
SCHOOL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
School Management System is an ideal way to manage complete school operation. This is for an efficient management of students, facilities, courses and classes while keeping a collaborative platform.
ACCOUNTING SYSTEM
Accounting System will help you manage your business’ records to track expenses, manage cashflows, invoices, and organize financial information that can be used to monitor, analyze, and improve operations.
CUSTOMER RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
CRM System will help in organizing and analyzing customer information data to better manage relationships with customers. This will allow you to track information across every touchpoint in a customer’s journey that can help you cater to their needs, build better relationships, and market smarter.
MARKETING AUTOMATION
Marketing Automation is a great help to save the time of your marketing team to carry out marketing-related operations, this will allow you to create multiple campaigns and set time. This will help your business to manage all campaign activities from a single platform, and easily track the flow of the campaign message and review success rate.
POINT OF SALE SYSTEM
POS System is centralized on sales transactions, payment processing, and customer relationship management. This system can be integrated with other system and get real-time control and accurate forecasts.
EVENT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
Event Management System will help you manage on-site and online events. It is an all-in-one event management platform that is capable of handling events of any type or scale. This will cover all aspects of an Event Planner’s job from event organization and ticket sales to visibility and promotion.
ONLINE SURVEY
Online Survey will be able to help you manage appraisals, satisfaction surveys, marketing campaigns, feedback forms, etc. You will be able to design and test your survey, share and collect data, analyze answers, live sessions, and immediately benefit from the results.
REAL PROPERTY TAX ASSESSMENT SYSTEM
Real Property Tax Assessment System will automate the assessment of taxes due on real estate properties. This will provide a comprehensive approach in maintaining large volume of property records and offers fast and accurate assessment service on all real property taxpayers for tax payment purposes.
DOCUMENT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
Document Management System is a centralized system that will help you in creating new documents, storing important files, managing recorded data, retrieval of documents, and their protection. You can store data in the forms of photos, videos, URLs, and PDF formats.
